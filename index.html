⚖ OpenBooks Arizona Fiscal Oversight — authorized vs. actual vs. audited. Leads, never findings.

—

Data contract: every tier, marker, and flag is a lead warranting confirmation — never a finding of fraud, misconduct, or illegality. No individual or entity is accused. Figures are cash-basis checkbook data and differ from audited GAAP (the ACFR governs). Authorized amounts are FY2025-26 appropriations (SB1847, first year of the biennium); actual spend is FY2025 checkbook (prior year) — fiscal-year and basis differences mean variance is a directional YoY signal, not a deficit or overage calculation. FY2022 is absent from source data; FY2019–2020 lack vendor/appropriation identifiers and under-flag by construction. "Machine-screened" means not yet reviewed — never clean. Analytics only; State of Arizona spending.
